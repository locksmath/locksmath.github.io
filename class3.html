Progressing from the KBA

Spring 2021

Progression from the KBA is one of the most fundamental skills in master keying-- and the one most commonly done incorrectly. Learn the correct way to progress pages from a Key Bitting Array, by hand or with master keying software. Learn to find and use incidental master keys and implement your key system plan.
