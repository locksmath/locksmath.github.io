Requirements for the AMKS Designation

If you wish to earn the designation of ALOA Master Keying Specialist, this course is the way to do that. Specifically, you must do each of the following:

  1. Attend all the classes.
  2. Pass all the quizzes.
  3. Complete all the homework.
  4. Pass the final exam.

You should receive an email when you have completed steps 1-3, informing you that you are eligible for step 4. At that point, you should contact the ALOA Education Office to schedule an appointment for you to take the final exam. education@aloa.org

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I take the final exam?
A: At the time of your appointment, you'll enter a video chat with a proctor, who will go over the instructions with you. When you are ready, the proctor will show you the exam questions. You do all your work sitting in front of a webcam, while the proctor watches, to verify that you aren't receiving any outside help. The maximum time is two hours. When you finish the exam, it will be sent to your instructor for grading. You should get an email within a few days, telling you whether you passed.

Q: If I don't pass the final exam, can I take it again?
Q: Yes. Contact the ALOA Education Office to schedule another appointment.

Q: What if I missed a few classes?
A: You may make up for what you missed by watching the video record of those classes. You may have received a link and password for the recordings, in your email. If you did not receive the link, or if you need it sent again, contact the ALOA Education Office.

Q: How do I know if I completed all the quizzes and homework?
A: At the end of the course, you should receive an email from your instructor, listing the assignments you have not completed (if any).

Q: What if I didn't pass one of the quizzes?
A: You may take any of the quizzes as many times as you like, until you obtain a passing score.

Q: What if I passed all the quizzes but some of my scores didn't get submitted?
A: Take a screen shot of your quiz score(s) and email it to your instructor.

Q: What's the deadline for completing all the homework?
A: There is no specific deadline, but it would be wise to submit all your homework within two to four weeks, while the lessons are still fresh in your mind.

Q: What's if some of my homework was marked as "please resubmit"?
A: If your homework contains a significant mistake, your instructor may ask you to correct it and resubmit. Your instructor may decide that your work overall is sufficient to consider it as "completed". You should receive an email at the end of the course, informing you what assignments are incomplete (if any). If you did not receive an email, of if you would like an update, please email your instructor.

Q: I emailed my instructor and got no response.
Q: Your instructor may have a long "to-do" list. Please be patient. If it's been more than five days, it's possible that your email was misplaced or that your instructor is unavailable. Contact the ALOA Education Office.
