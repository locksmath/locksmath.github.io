Expanding an Exhausted Master Key

Spring 2021

What can you do when you reach the last available change key and the customer still needs more? There are several techniques for squeezing change keys from under an exhausted master. The more techniques you know, the more likely you are to find one that will work in your system.

In this lesson, we practice the following skills:

  • Elevate the master key.
  • Rotate the constants (if any).
  • Re-use old change keys.
  • Going in-between ("Trick One")
  • Selective master keying IMKs ("Trick Two")
