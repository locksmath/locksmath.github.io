Prerequisites for Master Keying

Spring 2021
Before you begin a course in Master Keying, we assume that you know at least a little about locksmithing. If you have passed Fundamentals of Locksmithing, or if you have worked in a locksmith shop for at least six months, you probably know these things. Still, it couldn't hurt to review, and you might learn something.

Here are some general locksmithing terms that you should already know:
bitting
bottom pin
chamber
combinate, combination
control key
cylinder
driver pin
interchangeable core
KA = Keyed Alike
KD = Keyed Different
key control
key interchange
keyway
M.A.C.S.
operate
pin tumbler
plug
section
shear line
shell
