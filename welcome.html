Master Keying Fundamentals

Spring 2021
Welcome to the ALOA online Master Keying course. This is your opportunity to earn the AMKS designation: ALOA Master Keying Specialist.
This course is comprised of sixteen classes, running on Mondays and Thursdays from February 22 to April 15. For each class, there is a homework assignment, in two parts. The first part is a multiple-choice quiz, which you take online. The second part is a written assignment, which you will submit by email.
Sample Quiz Each quiz is multiple choice. When you click on your answer, it will immediately turn green (if you chose correctly), or red (if you chose incorrectly). At the end, you will get a score, and have the option to take the quiz again. There is no limit to how many times you can take the quiz. The score resets each time. Passing score is 70%. If you pass, you will have the option to submit your score to the instructor. If your score is 100%, you get a gold star. When you are ready, click "Start quiz."
