Common Mistakes in Master Keying

Spring 2021

Learn methods for dealing with worn out master key systems, master key systems that were not designed correctly, people trying to gain access to keys or areas they shouldn’t, product that shouldn’t be master keyed, systems of questionable origin, and other topics that make master keying risky for locksmiths and their customers. If you are a locksmith you have probably already encountered some of these issues. In this session you will learn to apply the master keying skills you have developed here to address these problems.
