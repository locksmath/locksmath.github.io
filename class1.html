Master Keying Fundamentals

Spring 2021
There are some important things that all locksmiths should know about Master Keying. In this lesson, we cover the mechanics of split pin master keying, what can go wrong, incidental master keys, different methods of master keying, construction master keying, multiplex keyways, and more. You will also learn the Eight Rules of Master Keying which will help keep you and your employees out of trouble.
