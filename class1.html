Master Keying Fundamentals
Welcome, ALOA Master Keying students. This
homework assignment has two parts. Part one
is multiple choice questions. When you click
on your answer, it will immediately turn green
if you choose correctly, or red if you chose
incorrectly. At the end, you will have the option
to either submit your score or start over. There
is no limit to how many times you can start
over. The score resets each time. When you are
ready, click "start".
