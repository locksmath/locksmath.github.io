Key Schematics and Keying Schedules

Spring 2021

Master Keying Schematics and Keying Schedules, described in ANSI A156.28, are the foundation of good record keeping and communication in master keying. Learn how to draw schematics using a computer or with paper and pen, and how to use keying schedules to create records and submittal documents.
