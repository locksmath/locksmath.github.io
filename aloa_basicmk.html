Master Keying Fundamentals

May 2021
Welcome to the ALOA online Master Keying Fundamentals course. This is a two part class which may help you prepare for the ALOA PRP Elective L30 Basic Master Keying. Part one is on Monday and part two is on Thursday. After Monday's class, there is a homework assignment, a multiple-choice quiz, which you may take live from this page.
Quiz Each quiz question is multiple choice. When you click on your answer, it will immediately turn green (if you chose correctly), or red (if you chose incorrectly). At the end, you will get a score, and have the option to take the quiz again. There is no limit to how many times you can take the quiz. The score resets each time. Passing score is 70%. If you pass, you will have the option to submit your score to the instructor. If your score is 100%, you get a gold star. When you are ready, click "Start quiz."
